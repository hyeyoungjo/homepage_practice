<Mesurer> is a media installation project.
It has two video, one projected on the wall and the other projected on the floor.
The former has 15 minutes long story about virtual character measuring physical and emotional distance between people.
The latter is a looped video of tapeline shrinking and stretching.
This was on display at Platform-L contemporary art center under the name of exhibiton <Lapses> curated by art collective group Eobchae.

<Dear. Young Me> is an installation project.
In the walls and corners of gallery, there are bubble gums containing tiny notes.
Each note has a sentimental melancholy sentence to recall childhood excitement.
As time goes by, the bubble gums deflate.
This was on display at Mythtake Museum under the name of exhibiton <Don’t fake it, believe it>.
